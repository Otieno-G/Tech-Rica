Tech Rica: Costa Rica’s Influence in the Tech Industry

Growth of the Tech Sector

Costa Rica has invested heavily in technology education and infrastructure.

The country exports over $3.3 billion in technology services annually and leads innovation in Latin America.

Global Tech Companies in Costa Rica

Major tech firms have established operations in Costa Rica, including Intel, IBM, and Amazon.

Costa Rica is home to research and development centers focused on AI, cloud computing, and cybersecurity.

Tech Education & Workforce Development

Costa Rica produces thousands of STEM graduates annually.

The country has a diverse and skilled workforce, with 37% of STEM graduates being women.
